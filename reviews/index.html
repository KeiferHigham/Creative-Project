Jeff, professinal food taster

Keifers Cafe was quite the experience, I had heard
good things about it from my colleagues, but I just
had to find out for myself. First of all let me just
say this, the views were out of this world!! I can't
think of a better view of the Maui Coast. I ordered
tuna steak and it did not dissapoint, It was obvious
that the Tuna was fresh, the sauce was incredible, a
a combination of seet with a little salty, the way it
it complemented the tuna was perfect. Overall I
would give Keifers Cafe a 9/10, I just told a couple
of my freinds about it, they definitely know what they
are doing here

James, food critique and
professional restaurant reviewer
with over 20 years of experience

I have spent alot of time touring the world reviewing
restaurants with an emphasis in seafood, so when it
comes to the overall quality of a seafood restuarant no one
is a harsher critic than me. That being said, I fell in
love with Keifers Cafe the second I stepped inside.
The architecture of the builiding combined with the views
was amazing. I ordered lobster burger, and absolutely
loved it. It was cooked perfectly and the taste and texture
of the lobster was incredible. The service was great, one
of the best experiences I've had trying out a restaurant in
a long time. I would give Keifers Cafe a 10/10.
